Telemetry & Haptic feedback Control Manual

Network Setup

For the system to receive telemetry, your Linux host must allow the following UDP communication:

# Inbound: Port 33740 (UDP) - Receives telemetry packets from GT7
# Outbound: Port 33739 (UDP) - Sends heartbeats to PS5/PS4 # Outbound: Port 5000 (TCP) - For web interface

Page 3 System Settings & Handles

This section explains every handle and toggle used to calibrate the tactile engine.

General Settings

  • PS5 IP Address: The network address used to find your console.
  • Master Volume: Main gain slider for overall shaker intensity. Recommended Setting: 90%
  • Screen Awake: Toggles browser wake lock to prevent display sleep. Please also check phone or tablet settings. This setting will not prevent screenlock if enabled on the unit
  • Light Mode UI: Changes between light and dark mode
  • Shaker Mode: Switch between 2 Shakers (Stereo Front/Rear) or 1 Shaker (Mono mixdown).
  • Audio Interface & Sample Rate: Select your soundcard and matching frequency (44.1/48kHz). Matching the hardware rate of your soundcard
  • Hardware Output Test: "Test Rear" and "Test Front" buttons to verify physical wiring. Buttons only active when shaker engine is stopped

Engine RPM Effect

Simulates the high-frequency vibration of a running engine.

  • Volume & Axle Balance: Sets the base vibration strength and balances it between front and rear axles. Recommended setting for volume 40%
  • Motor Profile: Choose between Sine (smooth), V8 (pulses), or Boxer (rhythmic) waveforms.
  • Min/Max Hz: Defines the frequency range of the engine vibrations. Recommended setting 35 - 100
  • Pit Boost (Idle Force): Increases shaker intensity when stationary to simulate a heavy idling engine.

Suspension Effects

Translates vertical wheel telemetry into physical motion.

  • Road Vol (Red): Volume for telemetry-driven road vibration.
  • Impact Vol (Blue): Volume for suspension thumps (curbs, jumps, crashes).
  • Road Sensitivity Sliders: Adjusts the "Threshold" for when road noise are triggered. Recommended setting: 0.2
  • Impact Sensitivity Sliders: Adjusts the "Threshold" for when impact noise are triggere like curbs. Recommended setting: 6
  • Priority suspension over engine effect: Briefly lowers engine volume during heavy impacts for better tactile clarity.

Traction Loss

Monitors tire slip by comparing wheel rotation to vehicle speed.

  • Volume: Intensity of the sliding vibration.
  • Slip Threshold: The sensitivity for when a slide is first detected.
  • Slip Sensitivity: Controls how quickly the vibration ramps up during a slide.
  • Autocalibration: Automatically optimizes slip detection for the current car's grip.
  • Front Frequency: Frequency adjustment for front axele. Recommended setting: 58hz.
  • Rear Frequency: Frequency adjustment for rear axele. Recommended setting: 35hz

Gear Shifts

Mechanical Thump upon gear shift

  • Function: Generates a sharp mechanical snap every time a gear change is detected.
  • Volume: Controls how aggressive the gear shift feels in the seat.

Simulated Road Texture

Uses speed-dependent noise to generate artificial tarmac texture.Bumbs and rougness hits frontaxle and then rear width the speed of the car. Calculated on a mid size car

  • Surface Texture (Vol): Volume control for surface texture. .
  • Surface Frequency: Adjusts the pitch (Hz) of the road rumble.
  • Surface Effect (Vol):Volume control for surface Roughness effects.
  • Surface Roughness: Adjusts the intensity of simulated cracks and unevenness.
